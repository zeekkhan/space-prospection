Delivery Information

Orders placed by 3 pm are posted on the same day.

We use the Evri postal network via our partners at HubEurope. We offer 2 delivery options:

  • Express, Next day, tracked (£8.99)
  • Economy, 2-4 day, tracked (£6.99)

Any order placed after 3 pm will be sent out the next working day at the latest. For example, if your order is placed on Friday at 7 pm, your order will be dispatched on Monday.

You will receive an email confirmation once you have placed your order, and a dispatch confirmation email upon the order being released for shipping. Evri will separately email you with a tracker and link to the latest updates/info.

Please note that all the above information is based on estimates/expectations. Due to our offshore location, issues such as adverse weather conditions or differing bank holidays may extend delivery times due to the disruption of outgoing boats/planes from the island. The majority of our orders are delivered in advance of the expectation, but there are cases where delays occur that are out of our control.
